Quick Scan: Do & Don’t

Do:

  • Use approved tools with the right data classifications.
  • Apply company frameworks and context when prompting.
  • Summarize chats monthly and store sanitized notes (exclude PII).
  • Reach out to GCO/IPLS when in doubt about IP or data.

Don’t:

  • Include personal or colleague PII (names, emails, phone numbers).
  • Input trade secrets, roadmaps, or marketing plans without approval.
  • Use third‑party IP externally without proper rights.
  • Rely on Copilot Chat for anything other than public research.

ChatGPT Capabilities & Tools

Explore the breadth of features available in ChatGPT Enterprise and see what's on the horizon. Badges indicate availability: blue for available, orange for pilot/beta, red for future roadmap.

Chat
Natural conversation, Q&A and summarization. Configure ChatGPT to act as a digital PM, generate user stories, acceptance criteria, PRDs and PR FAQs using tailored instructions. Great for drafting communications, status updates and competitive analyses.
Custom GPTs
Build personalized assistants that follow company frameworks (e.g., PRDs, PR FAQs) and enforce the right tone. Provide context and prompt templates so your Custom GPT can automate repetitive tasks, generate docs in various formats and deliver insights aligned with Amex standards.
Codex
Code generation and technical scripting assistant. Draft Python, SQL and CLI commands, debug and optimize code, and convert pseudocode into working prototypes. Enhance developer productivity across languages.
Canvas
A collaborative workspace where you can co‑edit documents and code with ChatGPT. Compare changes, restore previous versions and edit specific sections. Canvas includes version control and collaborative editing built directly into your AI workflow, so your team can iterate side by side without waiting for a pilot program.
Projects
Organize conversations by workstream, upload files, and maintain long‑term memory. Projects keep context across sessions, making it easy to iterate on designs, track decisions and collaborate with your team through the lifecycle of a product.
Scheduled Tasks
Automate reminders and periodic workflows. Set monthly tasks to summarize chats from the last 90 days and save sanitized learnings. Use scheduled tasks to fetch market updates, send meeting prep packages, or trigger analyses at regular intervals.
Agents
Fully autonomous workflows that can take multi‑step actions across your tools (e.g., update JIRA, draft an email and create a slide). Agents are on the roadmap and will enable “AI teammates” to handle busywork end‑to‑end.
Sora
A multimodal video generator that transforms text into short films and prototypes. Sora is still in development; imagine quickly pitching ideas with AI‑generated video storyboards and interactive demos.

Prompting Tips & Templates

Structured Output Template

  1. Role/Context: Describe who ChatGPT should be (e.g., “You are a Digital PM refining a user story…”).
  2. Task/Goal: Clearly state what you need (“Draft 5 acceptance criteria for this feature”).
  3. Format: Specify output style (“Present as a table with columns ID, Criteria, Priority”).
  4. Constraints: Add rules or extras (“Use plain language, avoid technical jargon”).
You are a Digital PM helping refine requirements. Given the following feature description, draft 5 acceptance criteria. Present them in a table with columns ID, Criteria, Priority. Keep criteria short and business‑friendly.

Research & Brainstorming Template

  1. Role: Set the creative mode (“You are an AI researcher exploring new product ideas…”).
  2. Task: Define the research question (“Brainstorm 3 innovative features for a travel app competing with Airbnb”).
  3. Output: Choose the format (“Provide bullet points with pros & cons and potential impact”).
  4. Extras: Include boundaries (“Consider market trends and competitor offerings; cite at least two sources if possible”).
You are an AI researcher exploring new product ideas. Brainstorm 3 innovative features for a travel app competing with Airbnb. Provide bullet points with pros & cons and potential impact. Consider market trends and competitor offerings.
